I'm Better Than You

Meow, meow meow.

Meow.

Meow meow, meow. Me-ow.

Meow.

Meow...

Meow.

(From the pronouncements of the ever-vigialent alley cat that parades down 15th Ave.)

This Is Why I Am Better Than You

  1. Meow meow meow, meow.
  2. Meow meow meow meeeeeow.
  3. MEOW. (Meow.)
  4. Meow meow meowmeowmeow.
  5. Meow, meow, meow.

(The inevitable sequal to his first work, as transcribed from its first performance atop the local ball park fence.)

Where Is My Food?

Meow meow meow meow meow! Meow meow!

Meow meow?

Meow, meow. Meow meow.

Meow meow meow meow, meow meow meow?

Meow!!!

Meow meow meow, meow meow meow, meow meow meow.

Meow meow meow meow, "Meow meow?"

Meow.

(A call to arms, a true political treatise, and his first step beyond mere critism of the faults of man to the revolutionary tones that come to define his career.)
